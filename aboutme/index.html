About Me

Jared Goff

I am from Kaysville Utah. Born and raised here and am currently raising our children here. I am married to Eloina Guerrero Goff for 27 years.
Married in the Bountiful Temple. Our children Carlos (24) Emilia (15) and Cristina (14). Ostia is our best friend.
Carlos and I love to watch BYU football together. Eloina and I met at Pizza Hut where we were both Managers.

Eloina is teaching Spanish full time at the local Junior High. I work for the Utah Olympic Legacy Foundation as the Director of IT for the past 14 years.
The Foundation is responsible for management of the three Major Olympic Venues constructed for the Salt Lake 2002 Winter Olympics.
Utah Olympic Park, Utah Olympic Oval, and Soldier Hollow Nordic Center. I love bringing my family to major sporting events hosted at these venues.
I also enjoying working at these venues. It is very satisfying when our athletes win medals at the World Cups or the Olympics
knowing that you had a small part in helping them achieve their dreams.

I am CIT major. BYU-I has giving me the opportunity to return to school and finish my degree. I will forever be grateful for this.
